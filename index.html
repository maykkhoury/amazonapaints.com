About us
About us

Amazona is a leading company specialized in the development and production of paints and coatings in Lebanon, the Middle East and Africa.

Innovating Excellence in Paints and Coatings

Amazona Paints is a trailblazer in the development and production of high-quality paints and coatings, proudly serving Lebanon, the Middle East, and Africa. With over three decades of experience, our legacy has been built on a foundation of unparalleled craftsmanship, relentless innovation, and unwavering dedication to our clients.

Since our establishment in 1984, Amazona has consistently grown, expanding our reach and influence across multinational markets, even amidst fierce competition. Our reputation for excellence is anchored in our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction, making us the go-to brand for discerning clients.

At Amazona, we pride ourselves on our exceptional blend of premium raw materials, cutting-edge equipment, and a highly skilled team of engineers and technical staff. Our extensive research and innovative approach fuel our ability to create custom-made solutions that exceed client expectations.

Our passion for quality has earned us the prestigious ISO 9001:2015 certification for quality management, showcasing our unyielding dedication to setting the benchmark in the paints and coatings industry.

Discover Amazona Paints – where exceptional quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction converge to create a vibrant world of color.

Over 35 Years of Excellence, Innovation, and Sustainability

At Amazona Paints, our rich history spans over 35 years, during which we have cultivated an extensive portfolio of over 100 diverse and innovative products. Our unwavering commitment to research, development, and constant improvement has enabled us to stay ahead in the industry and cater to the ever-evolving needs of our clients.

We prioritize customer satisfaction and maintain a keen focus on responsiveness to customer feedback, ensuring we deliver the best solutions for their unique requirements. Upholding the highest standards and certifications, we strive to be the benchmark in the paints and coatings market.
