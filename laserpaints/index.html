About

About us
About us

Amazona is a leading company specialized in the development and production of paints and coatings in Lebanon and the Middle East.

Established in 1984, Amazona has been able to build up a long list of references on a multinational level.

Despite strong competition the company has shown fast growth and expansion by building a reputation based on quality and client satisfaction.

Amazona achieved excellent combination of high quality raw materials, highly qualified engineers and technical staff, intensive research, advanced equipment and innovation.

By custom made products, Amazona endeavors to meet client satisfaction.

Amazona’s strong commitment to quality has resulted in acquiring the ISO 9001: 2015 certificate for quality management.
